# RAG Query: Who is Saraswati Thakur?

## Answer

He is also described as a senior-most sannyasī and a great divine master, and is considered the protector of pure devotion and the dearmost of Śrī Saraswatī Ṭhākur. Additionally, Srila B.R. Sridhar Maharaj refers to him as "Nayana-maṇi," indicating that the internal name for Saraswatī Ṭhākur is 'Nayana-maṇi'. Therefore, Saraswati Thakur, in the context provided, is a revered spiritual master embodying Gaurāṅga's mercy and representing the highest form of Vaiṣṇava devotion, with an internal name of 'Nayana-maṇi' and a role as a protector and embodiment of pure devotion.
