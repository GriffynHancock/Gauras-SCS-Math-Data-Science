# RAG Query: Who is Mahaprabhu according to Sridhar Maharaj?

## Answer
 According to Srila B.R. Sridhar Maharaj, Mahaprabhu is the Lord of love, who lives on prema (love), and is love personified. He is described as being very greedy to eat this love, and love is the inner existence of all of us. Mahaprabhu is also referred to as the Supreme Personality of Godhead, and is seen as the source of all spiritual realization and devotion. He is the one who is approached by devotees through surrender and the practice of devotional service (bhakti), which is considered to be the highest path to spiritual life. In the teachings of Srila B.R. Sridhar Maharaj, Mahaprabhu is also seen as the one who conquers and captures the Kāzī, leading a saṅkīrtan party and demonstrating the power and effectiveness of devotion. Additionally, Mahaprabhu is viewed as being the central figure in the Gaudiya Vaishnava tradition, through whom the teachings of Śrī Chaitanya Deva are propagated.
